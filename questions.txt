3.How are unit tests different from integration and end-to-end testing.
    -E2E (end-to-end) testing is a type of functional test. Unlike a unit test, you're not breaking the application down into smaller parts in order to test it - you're testing the entire application. E2E tests interact with your app just like a real user would.
